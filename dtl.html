Rules

You must prepare a powerpoint (or similar) presentation no longer than three (3) minutes in length.

The deadline to RSVP (including submitting your presentation title) is 11:59pm Pacific, December 28th 2022.

If your presentation exceeds three (3) minutes, you will be required to finish your drink, then continue your presentation. Every additional minute over will incur additional alcohol-based penalties.

Heckling is allowed if it is clever and brief. Heckling deemed not to be clever will be punished.

Setting your slides on a timer to ensure you're under three (3) minutes is cheating, and thus not allowed.

The dress code is Business Casual, but there is an award for best dressed, so keep that in mind.

Slideshows will run off a computer we provide, so please submit them by the deadline (or at least 1 day in advance) of the event so we can ensure things go smoothly.
